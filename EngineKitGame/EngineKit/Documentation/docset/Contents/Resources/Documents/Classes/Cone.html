  topRadius

An alias for the SCNCone’s @p topRadius property. Speficies the radius used for the Cone’s top circle when it is a conical frustrum. A top radius of 0 is the default and results in an ordinary cone. The top circle is aligned with the @p xz plane and is centered in the origin.
